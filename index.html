Dish of the Week

Bún Riêu

Indulge in the rich and flavorful world of Vietnamese cuisine with our weekly special: Bún Riêu. This hearty noodle soup features a tangy, spicy broth infused with the essence of tomato, tamarind, and chili peppers. Delicate rice vermicelli noodles intertwine with a medley of seafood, including succulent shrimp and crab, adding a delightful texture and briny taste. For a unique experience, savor the addition of blood tofu, a soft and savory ingredient that complements the other flavors perfectly. To complete this culinary masterpiece, we have garnished the Bún Riêu with a variety of fresh herbs, providing a burst of aromatic goodness. Whether you're a seasoned Vietnamese food enthusiast or simply looking to explore new flavors, Bún Riêu is sure to satisfy your cravings and leave you wanting more.
